Our Story

Founded in 2015, SchoolSys began as a small project to help local schools manage their operations more efficiently.

What started as a simple student information system has grown into a comprehensive platform serving over 1,200 schools across 15 countries. Our team of educators and technologists work together to create solutions that address real challenges in school administration.

Today, we're proud to be recognized as one of the most innovative school management platforms, constantly evolving to meet the changing needs of educational institutions.
